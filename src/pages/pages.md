Page components (routes)

Contains page-level components (typically mapped to routes) that use the components from /features or /components.
Example:

/pages
    ├── Auth/
    │   └── SignInPage.tsx
    │   └── SignUpPage.tsx
  ├── Dashboard.tsx
  ├── Home.tsx
  ├── Users.tsx
  ├── Prodcuts.tsx
  └── ContactUs.tsx
